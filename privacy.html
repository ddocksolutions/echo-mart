Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Echo Mart, operated by Ddocksolutions, collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information when users access the Echo Mart mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of user data:

  • Personal information such as name, phone number, and email address
  • Delivery address and location details provided by the user
  • Order history and transaction details
  • Device and app usage data for performance, analytics, and security

2. Use of Collected Information

User data is used for the following purposes:

  • Processing and delivering orders
  • Facilitating payments, refunds, and customer support
  • Improving application performance and user experience
  • Communicating important service-related updates

3. Data Storage and Security

We store user data securely using industry-standard security measures. Reasonable safeguards are implemented to protect data against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

4. Data Sharing

User information may be shared only with:

  • Partner vendors and delivery personnel to fulfill orders
  • Third-party payment service providers such as PhonePe
  • Government or legal authorities when required by law

We do not sell or rent user data to third parties.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Echo Mart may use cookies or similar technologies to:

  • Maintain user session and preferences
  • Analyze app usage for performance improvements
  • Enhance security and prevent fraudulent activity

Users may manage or disable cookies through their device or browser settings, which may affect certain app features.

6. User Rights

Users have the right to:

  • Access or update their personal information
  • Request deletion of their account, subject to legal obligations
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Echo Mart reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Continued use of the application constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
