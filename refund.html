Refund Policy

This Refund Policy outlines the guidelines and procedures for order cancellations, refunds, and replacements on the Echo Mart mobile application, operated by Ddocksolutions.

1. Refund Eligibility

Users may be eligible for a refund in the following situations:

  • Order cancelled within the permitted cancellation window
  • Item not available or order not fulfilled
  • Incorrect, damaged, or expired items delivered
  • Failed, duplicate, or excess payment transactions

2. Non-Refundable / Non-Cancellable Items

Certain products, including perishable goods (such as fresh fruits, vegetables, meat, or dairy items), may not be eligible for return or cancellation once delivered, unless damaged or incorrect.

3. Cancellation Process

Orders may be cancelled through the app before dispatch or order processing begins. Once an order has been dispatched or prepared by the store, cancellation may not be permitted.

4. Return / Replacement Requests

In case of damaged or incorrect items, users must raise a return or replacement request through the app or by contacting customer support within the specified time after delivery.

5. Refund Method

Approved refunds will be processed to the original payment method used at the time of purchase. Echo Mart does not provide cash refunds.
